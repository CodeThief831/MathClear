# Code of Conduct

MathClear is a learning project for students who may already feel anxious about examinations and backlogs.

Contributors must communicate respectfully, welcome beginners, accept correction professionally and avoid ridicule based on marks, backlogs, language or mathematical ability.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexual content, deliberate misinformation and publication of private information are not accepted.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. The owner may edit, remove or reject contributions and restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.
